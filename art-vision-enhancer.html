Art Vision Enhancer Chatbot

Cambridge Research Project

Project Overview

The aim of this project was to develop a chatbot capable of engaging with students on their art concepts, encouraging deeper exploration and creativity through intelligent questioning and dialogue.

Key Features

  • Creative Dialogue System: Engages students in meaningful conversations about their art concepts through adaptive questioning.
  • Concept Exploration: Encourages deeper thinking by prompting students to expand on their initial ideas and consider new perspectives.
  • Natural Language Processing: Interprets student responses with advanced NLP techniques for more human-like interactions.
  • Adaptive Learning: The chatbot's questioning mechanism evolves based on student responses to maintain engaging dialogue.
  • Artistic Inspiration: Provides thoughtful prompts and suggestions to stimulate creative thinking.

Technologies Used

Core Framework
Django for backend implementation
Natural Language Processing
ChatGPT-4 API for dialogue generation and understanding
Machine Learning
Custom algorithms for adaptive questioning and response analysis
Frontend
HTML5/CSS3 interface with responsive design
Concept Inspiration
Art education theories, creative cognition, and dialogue systems

Project Impact

This innovative chatbot demonstrates how AI can enhance creative education by:

  • Personalised Learning: Adapts to each student's unique creative process and thinking style
  • Accessibility: Provides art education support outside traditional classroom settings
  • Creative Stimulation: Helps overcome creative blocks through intelligent questioning
  • Research Potential: Offers insights into how AI can support arts education and creative development
